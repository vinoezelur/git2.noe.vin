This was taken during a trip to Vietnam, during the dairy revolution in my native street. I was working closely with **Linghi Chetty Street Council for Milk and Milk products (LCSCMMP)**, to check if we can milk elephants to combat the local shortages. As a citizen and integral part of the society, I took it upon myself to verify it first hand. The elephant looks less lively cause she was stoned.
